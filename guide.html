📘 VibeCommentary Guide

This extension helps you write smart and personalized LinkedIn comments using OpenAI. Here’s how to get the most out of it.

1. 🔑 Add your OpenAI API Key

Go to Settings and paste your API key. You can get one from OpenAI’s dashboard.

2. 🎯 Choose Your Default Tone

In Settings, you can select your favorite tone and even customize the prompt and guideline for each tone type like:

  • Friendly – warm and casual
  • Contrarian – add new angles to a post
  • Ask a Question – spark a conversation
  • ...and many more

3. 💬 Use the “Suggest Comments” Button

Go to any LinkedIn post. Click on the blue “💬 Suggest Comments” button that appears below it. It will open a modal with comment ideas, tone switcher, DM suggestion, and an optional “Improve your own comment” area.

4. ✍️ Improve Your Comment

Inside the modal, write your comment in the box, click “✨ Improve” and get a better version instantly. You can customize its behavior using the “Improve” tone settings.

5. ⚙️ Export & Import Settings

Want to reuse your tone prompts and settings later? Use the Export Settings and Import Settings buttons on the Settings page.
